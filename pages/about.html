Who owns this site?

Buy Bitcoin Worldwide was founded by Jordan Tuwiner in June 2015. Jordan discovered Bitcoin in 2013 and has been helping others learn about Bitcoin since.

Jordan had helped friends buy bitcoins. He realized all of them had the same basic questions:

  • what is Bitcoin?
  • how do I buy bitcoin?

He used to have to site down with his friends and show people how to send a bank transfer to a Bitcoin exchange.

It was clear that people wanted to learn, but a high quality, easy to understand resource was missing. So, Jordan built something to help the rest of the world buy Bitcoin and Buy Bitcoin Worldwide was created!
